## Key Operational Considerations

- **Safety First**: All operations must prioritize personnel safety
- **Well Control**: Maintain constant vigilance for kicks
- **Hole Condition**: Monitor and maintain wellbore integrity
- **Efficiency**: Minimize NPT while maintaining quality
- **Documentation**: Accurate, timely reporting
