## NOTIFICATION PROTOCOLS

### Incident Notification Chain

```
IMMEDIATE NOTIFICATIONS (Within 15 Minutes)
├── 911 (if emergency services needed)
├── Toolpusher
├── Company Man
└── Drilling Superintendent

URGENT NOTIFICATIONS (Within 1 Hour)
├── HSE Manager
├── District Manager
├── Regulatory (if required)
└── Corporate Security (if applicable)

FOLLOW-UP NOTIFICATIONS (Within 24 Hours)
├── Complete incident report
├── Insurance notification
├── Contractor management
└── Environmental (if applicable)
```

### Reportable Incidents

| Incident Type | Report To | Time Frame |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Fatality | OSHA, State | Immediate (8 hrs) |
| Hospitalization | OSHA, State | 24 hours |
| Blowout | BSEE, State | Immediate |
| Spill (reportable) | NRC, State | Immediate |
