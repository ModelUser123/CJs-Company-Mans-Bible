## HPHT Planning

### Well Design Considerations

```
HPHT CASING DESIGN
├── Higher Design Factors
│   ├── Burst: 1.25+ (vs 1.1 conventional)
│   ├── Collapse: 1.25+ (vs 1.0)
│   ├── Tension: 1.5+ (vs 1.3)
│   └── Connection strength verified
├── Temperature Derating
│   ├── Steel yield strength decreases
│   ├── ~5% per 100°F above 300°F
│   ├── Use derated values in design
│   └── Consider production temps
├── Connection Selection
│   ├── Premium connections required
│   ├── Metal-to-metal seal
│   ├── Gas tight rating
│   ├── Temperature rated
│   └── Torque shoulder design
├── Special Considerations

│   ├── Thermal expansion
│   ├── Trapped annular fluids

│   └── Burst rupture disks

    ├── Higher grade steels

    └── Elastomer ratings
```
