# CT Intervention Operations

## Wellbore Cleanouts

### Sand/Fill Cleanout

```
SAND CLEANOUT PROCEDURE
├── Pre-Job
│   ├── Know fill depth/volume
│   ├── Select CT size (match annulus)
│   ├── BHA: Jetting nozzle/motor
│   ├── Fluids: Clean, appropriate weight
│   └── Calculate circulation rates
├── Execution
│   ├── RIH to top of fill
│   ├── Start circulation
│   ├── Slowly work into fill
│   ├── Maintain returns
│   ├── Watch for bridging
│   └── Continue to TD
├── BHA Options
│   ├── Jet nozzle (simple, effective)
│   ├── Jetting motor (adds rotation)
│   ├── Milling motor (hard fill)
│   └── Combination tools
├── Fluid Selection
│   ├── Clean brine (most common)
│   ├── Weighted fluid (if needed)
│   ├── Nitrogen assist (foam)
│   └── Compatible with formation
└── Success Indicators
    ├── Consistent returns
    ├── Decreasing sand at surface
    ├── Reach target depth
    └── Clean circulation at TD
```

### Cleanout Parameters

| Parameter | Sand Cleanout | Scale Removal | Paraffin |
|-----------|--------------|---------------|----------|
| CT Size | 1.5-2" | 1.5-2.375" | 1.5-2" |
| Rate | 0.5-2 bpm | 0.5-1.5 bpm | 0.5-1 bpm |
| Fluid | Brine | Acid/solvent | Hot oil |
| Speed | 10-30 ft/min | 5-20 ft/min | 20-50 ft/min |
