## Cost Control Overview

### Company Man Cost Responsibilities

```
COST MANAGEMENT DUTIES
├── Daily Tracking
│   ├── Record all daily expenses
│   ├── Verify contractor invoices
│   ├── Document NPT and causes
│   ├── Track against AFE
│   └── Report significant variances
├── Vendor Management
│   ├── Verify work performed
│   ├── Approve field tickets
│   ├── Document equipment times
│   ├── Monitor performance
│   └── Address issues immediately
├── AFE Tracking
│   ├── Compare actual to budget
│   ├── Identify variances early
│   ├── Request AFE supplements if needed
│   ├── Forecast final costs
│   └── Communicate to management
└── Optimization
    ├── Minimize NPT
    ├── Identify inefficiencies
    ├── Suggest improvements
    └── Balance cost vs quality
```

### Typical Well Cost Components

| Category | % of Well Cost | Key Items |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| Rig | 25-40% | Day rate, fuel, water |
| Services | 25-35% | Mud, cement, logging, directional |
| Tubulars | 15-25% | Casing, tubing, drill pipe |

| Other | 5-15% | Fuel, rentals, supervision |

## Key Metrics

### Efficiency Metrics

| Metric | Calculation | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Cost/foot | Total cost ÷ TD | Minimize |
| Cost/day | Daily spread cost | Per budget |
| ROP | Feet drilled ÷ hours | Maximize |
| Drilling % | Drill time ÷ Total time | >70% |
| NPT % | NPT hours ÷ Total hours | <10% |
| Connection time | Minutes per connection | Minimize |

### Variance Thresholds

| Variance | Action Required |
|----------|----------------|
| <5% | Normal, document |
| 5-10% | Investigate, report |
| 10-20% | Management notification |
| >20% | AFE supplement discussion |

---

*Effective cost control requires daily attention to detail. Document everything and communicate variances early.*
