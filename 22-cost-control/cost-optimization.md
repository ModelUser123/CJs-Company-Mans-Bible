# Cost Optimization Strategies

## Cost Reduction Philosophy

### Optimization vs Cost Cutting

```
OPTIMIZATION APPROACH
├── Cost Reduction Goals
│   ├── Reduce without compromising safety
│   ├── Improve efficiency, not just cut spending
│   ├── Focus on value, not just price
│   └── Sustainable, repeatable savings
├── Key Principles
│   ├── Never compromise safety for cost
│   ├── Quality work prevents rework costs
│   ├── Planning prevents expensive problems
│   ├── Time IS money - efficiency matters
│   └── Small daily savings compound significantly
├── Areas of Focus
│   ├── Rig time (largest cost driver)
│   ├── NPT reduction
│   ├── Service company efficiency
│   ├── Material/consumable usage
│   └── Logistics optimization
└── Mindset
    ├── Every hour costs $1,000-4,000+
    ├── Every decision has cost impact
    ├── Proactive beats reactive
    └── Measure to manage
```

## NPT Prevention

### NPT Categories and Prevention

| NPT Type | Typical Cost | Prevention Strategy |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| Stuck pipe | $50k-500k+ | Hole cleaning, practices |
| Lost circulation | $20k-200k | LCM ready, proper MW |
| Kick/well control | $50k-1M+ | Training, procedures |
| Equipment failure | $10k-100k+ | Maintenance, spares |
| Twist-off | $50k-300k+ | Inspection, parameters |
| Weather delay | Variable | Planning, forecasting |
| Waiting on equipment | $10k-100k+ | Logistics planning |
