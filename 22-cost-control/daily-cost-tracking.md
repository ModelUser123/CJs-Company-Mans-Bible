# Daily Cost Tracking

## Field Ticket Management

### Field Ticket Verification

```
FIELD TICKET REVIEW PROCESS
├── Receive Ticket
│   ├── From contractor each day
│   ├── Or at job completion
│   ├── Before they leave location
│   └── Timely review critical
├── Verify Information
│   ├── Date and time correct
│   ├── Well name/API number
│   ├── Contractor information
│   ├── Work description accurate
│   └── Personnel/equipment listed
├── Verify Work Performed
│   ├── Did work actually occur?
│   ├── Match to DDR activities
│   ├── Equipment on location?
│   ├── Hours reasonable?
│   └── Materials used?
├── Verify Pricing
│   ├── Match to contract rates
│   ├── Correct billing codes
│   ├── No duplicate charges
│   ├── Discounts applied
│   └── Unit prices correct
└── Approve or Dispute
    ├── Sign if correct
    ├── Note any adjustments
    ├── Dispute in writing
    └── Resolve before leaving
```

### Common Ticket Issues

| Issue | Action | Prevention |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Wrong dates | Correct before signing | Verify daily |
| Hours inflated | Reduce to actual | Monitor closely |
| Wrong well | Reject, resubmit | Clear identification |
| Unbundled charges | Verify contract | Know contract |
| Duplicate billing | Delete duplicate | Track all tickets |
| Non-contracted rate | Apply correct rate | Know contract |

## Vendor Payment Approval

### Approval Process

```
VENDOR PAYMENT APPROVAL
├── Field Level (Company Man)
│   ├── Verify work performed
│   ├── Verify rates per contract
│   ├── Sign field tickets
│   ├── Note any disputes
│   └── Forward to office
├── Office Level
│   ├── Enter into tracking system
│   ├── Match to AFE line item
│   ├── Approval routing
│   └── Payment processing
├── Dispute Resolution
│   ├── Note dispute on ticket
│   ├── Contact vendor
│   ├── Negotiate resolution
│   ├── Document agreement
│   └── Adjust payment
└── Documentation
    ├── Keep copies of all tickets
    ├── Maintain daily cost sheets
    ├── File by date and vendor
    └── Support any audits
```

### Payment Authorization Levels

| Amount | Approval Required |
|--------|------------------|
| <$5,000 | Company Man |
| $5,000-$25,000 | Drilling Superintendent |
| $25,000-$100,000 | District Manager |
| >$100,000 | VP/Senior Management |

---

*Accurate daily cost tracking is essential for AFE management and well economics. Document all costs daily - don't rely on memory.*
